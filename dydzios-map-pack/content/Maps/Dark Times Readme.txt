Dark Times

By Scrowe
simon_crowe@excite.com

Players: 1 human, 3 computer
Size: Medium
Difficulty: Hard
Special Victory Conditions: Capture Dark Hole

Description:
You return to Haranroth to find evil festering at its core, can you save the lands? You must search every inch of the terrain for whatever help you can find, but beware for evil has rotted the lands and lurks everywhere.

Notes:
My first real single player map, and my favourite. I therefore would appreciate any feed back you can give me.
